Entity - Relationship Model

A logical design method which emphasizes simplicity and readability. Basic objects of the model are:Entities,Relationships,Attributes

Entities

Data objects detailed by the information in the database. Denoted by rectangles in the model.

Attributes

Characteristics of entities or relationships. Denoted by ellipses in the model.

Relationships

Represent associations between entities. Denoted by diamonds in the model.
