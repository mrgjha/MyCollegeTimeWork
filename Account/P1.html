Accounts (Personal, Real, Nominal)

Personal (Natural (creation of god) 
Artificial (corporate bodies or institutions as persons), 
Representive Personal 
(represent a certain group or group of persons))

Debit the receiver and credit the giver


Real(Tangible (things can be seen, touched or felt,physically), 
Intangible (things can’t be touched))

Debit what comes in, and credit what goes out


Nominal (nature of transactions)
(Expenses and gains, Incomes and gains)

Debit all expenses and loses and 
credit all incomes and gains
